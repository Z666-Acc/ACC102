# Stock Health Diagnostic Tool

A free, interactive tool for retail investors to analyse, compare, and simulate stock portfolios. No finance degree required.

## What Is This Tool For?

Are you trying to decide which stocks are worth your attention, but overwhelmed by complex financial jargon? This tool gives you a clear, data-driven picture of any stock's performance in plain English. Whether you are a first-time investor or just looking for a smarter way to compare stocks, this tool does the heavy lifting for you.

## What Can You Do With It?

**Get an Instant Stock Health Score**

Every stock gets a score from 0 to 100 based on its return, risk, and efficiency. You will also get an automatic investment profile — Aggressive/Growth, Balanced/Moderate, or Conservative/Defensive — so you know at a glance what kind of stock you are looking at.

**Compare Stocks Against the Market**

See how your chosen stocks perform against major benchmarks like the S&P 500, NASDAQ, or Dow Jones, and find out which ones actually beat the market.

**Understand the Real Risks**

Go beyond simple price charts. See how volatile a stock has been over time, the worst drop it has ever experienced, and whether its returns carry hidden tail risks.

**Spot Diversification Opportunities**

The correlation heatmap shows you which stocks move together and which do not, helping you build a portfolio that does not all fall at the same time.

**Build and Test Your Own Portfolio**

Use the Portfolio Simulator to drag sliders and set how much you invest in each stock. Instantly see your combined portfolio's return, risk, and Sharpe ratio, and compare your growth against a benchmark index.

**See a 30-Day Price Trend Forecast**

Get a simple linear trend forecast for any stock over the next 30 trading days, so you can visualise where prices might be heading based on historical momentum.

## How To Use It

1. Enter stock tickers in the sidebar (e.g. AAPL, MSFT, NVDA, TSLA)
2. Choose your date range and analyse any period from 2010 to today
3. Select a benchmark to compare against (S&P 500, NASDAQ, or Dow Jones)
4. Explore the tabs, each one gives you a different angle on the data
5. Use the Portfolio Simulator to test your own investment mix

All data is pulled live from Yahoo Finance, free, reliable, and always up to date.

## Disclaimer

This tool is designed for educational purposes only. All scores, forecasts, and analysis are based on historical data and simplified financial models. Nothing presented here constitutes financial advice. Always consult a qualified financial professional before making investment decisions.
